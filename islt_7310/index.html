Matthew R. Dieter

Educator and instructional designer with a passion for creating
materials and environments that help learners achieve success

I am an experienced educator who has served as a classroom teacher, school administrator and an undergraduate professor. As the instructional leader at my school, I have worked collaboratively with staff members to help them design and assess instruction for their students. I have designed and delivered multiple professional learning opportunities for the faculty members I supervise in using technology following the SAMR model to enhance their instructional practices. I have experience using both Canvas and Moodle as a learning management system and have taught my students using both face-to-face and online class sessions. I have experience using a variety of software for presenting and creating instructional materials.
